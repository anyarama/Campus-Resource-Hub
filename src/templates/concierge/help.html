What is the Resource Concierge?

The AI Resource Concierge is an intelligent search assistant that understands natural language queries. Instead of filling out complex search forms, just tell me what you need in plain English!

How to Use It

  1. Describe what you need - Use natural language like you're talking to a person
  2. Be specific if you can - Mention capacity, dates, times, or locations
  3. Review the results - I'll show you matching resources with a conversational explanation
  4. Click to book - When you find what you need, click"View Details" to book it
